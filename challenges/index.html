Challenges

Stay clear, danger near

#toxicity #zones #geotagging #design #datavis #imageprocessing #GoogleGlass

For astronauts to know about the dangers surrounding them currently, they must actively read and scan labels without information on how to properly handle the materials.

Help build a system that will:

  • Collect information about dangers surrounding a person
  • Communicate these dangers to the person in any manner

Find out more!

Swiss Army Knife for Imaging

#imaging #hardware #visual

The delivery of in-flight medical care to astronauts is limited by the scope of diagnostic and treatment resources on board the vehicle and the medical knowledge of the crewmembers. Design a “multiscope” that can image multiple body parts within the visible portion of the electromagnetic spectrum for ground medical support.

Find out more!

Saving Time to Save Lives

#design #SOP #procedures #DataRepresentation #language #RFP

For emergency situations in space, every second is crucial.
Currently, the Emergency Operations Procedures for Space are in an instruction manual format that is difficult to decipher.

Create an interface to guide astronauts through standard protocols used in response to emergency situations to enable smooth execution and minimized hesitation.

Find out more!

No-Contact Heart Rate Monitor

#imageprocessing #pulse #webcam #python #quantifiedself

Currently, getting heart rate requires special equipment. Help uncover heart rate in video logs by improving the webcam pulse detector.

Find out more!

Medical HAL 3000

#medicaldata #interface #softwaredesign

Health information of astronauts in space is important both for health maintenance/treatment and medical research of the human body in space. How do we acquire, integrate, analyze, store and communicate health data in space efficiently and effectively?

Find out more!

Intergalactic Traveling University

#VirtualReality #CBT #datavis #DistanceLearning #education #communication #ImageProcessing

Some medical training for astronauts can only be obtained by reading PDFs and is therefore unengaging. Build an interactive system to help astronauts with training and refreshers while in space.

Find out more!

I Spy...

#audio #RFID #ImageProcessing #AugmentedReality #GoogleGlass

With the lack of gravity and the amount of things that get sent into space, tools and items are easily lost. This is especially a problem during high pressure situations.

Develop an application to automatically locate misplaced items on the space station via advanced video processing.

Find out more!

Earth-Space Communication Simulation

#simulation #communication #network

Earth-space communication can be laggy, staticky, noisy and subject to other distortion. How do we anticipate these problems and test systems built on Earth under space conditions?

Simulate or build a lan/wireless network that mimics the limitations in communication between Earth/Mars (~20 min delay). Open-source this tool on GitHub.

Find out more!
