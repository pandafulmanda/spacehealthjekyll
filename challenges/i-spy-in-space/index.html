I Spy...

#audio #RFID #ImageProcessing #AugmentedReality #GoogleGlass

The Problem

Locating lost items

With the lack of gravity and the amount of things that get sent into space, tools and items are easily lost. This is especially a problem during high pressure situations.

The Mission

Develop an application to automatically locate misplaced items on the space station via advanced video processing. Think of how in sci-fi movies, robots are able to scan the scene and different objects in view get identified and are displayed on screen along with details .

Think of this as an automated "I spy..."

Mission Breakdown

Helpful hints

  • All items have a barcode
  • Could you use infrared, image processing, RFID and others to detect and differentiate items?
  • Identify one specific object as a demo. If just a portion of item is visible, can that still be tagged/identified?
  • Demo a proof of concept with static images

Possible approaches

  • Speech input to query a search for an item
  • Handheld cameras or scanners as the hardware interface
  • Google Glass as the hardware interface
